How to Use Text-Utils App

Description:
Text-Utils is a web app made with Django that helps you analyze and change text quickly. You can count words and characters, remove extra spaces, and convert text to uppercase or lowercase.

Instructions to Use:

Open the app in your web browser.

Enter the text you want to work with.

Choose the action you want:

Count words/characters

Remove extra spaces

Convert to UPPERCASE or lowercase

Click the button, and see the result immediately.
